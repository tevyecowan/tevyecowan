Who I am

I believe that by using an interdisciplinary and socially just approach, we can use Information Technology to better our world. I am inspired by the growing movement in tech to analyse the social implications of one's code and projects, and to infuse ethics into all parts of the development process.

My previous academic scholarship in Religious Studies has contributed to my interest in interdisciplinary approaches to solving problems, and my current education in Information and Computer Systems is providing me with the programming skills needed to solve those problems.
